History

Today's modern game of golf originated in 15th-century Scotland. It spread into the United Kingdom and the British Empire before making its way into the United States in the 19th century. Throughout history, golf, or similar versions of golf, have been banned for various reasons. The oldest playing golf course in the world is The Old Links in Musselburgh. First played in 1672. Golf is a fun sport enjoyed by all ages, male and female, all around the world.

Golf Formats

  • Stroke Play: This is the most common format of golf. It's an 18 hole cumulative score tournament where the goal is to get the lowest score. Stroke play is you against the course in reality. To win you just have to score lower than the other competitors.
  • Match Play: This is a hole by hole tournament. Your goal is to score lower than your opponent on each individual hole. The player who wins the most holes is declared the winner. Consider this a head to head competition.
  • Best Ball: One of my favorite formats, best ball is your team (up to 4 total players) against an opponent. Best ball can be played with stroke play or match play format rules. Each player plays their own ball for the entire hole and the lowest score between your team is kept. So if you play poorly on a hole, chances are your teammates score will be the once kept.
  • Scramble: Similar to best ball, each team member (up to 4) plays their own ball. However, after each shot, the team gets to choose the best shot. Every player on the team then plays their next shot from that spot. Again, if you hit a bad shot and a teammate hits a better one then you play your next shot from where your teammates ball landed. Your team only counts one stroke from each shot.
  • Skins: Skins format golf is played individually or with a team. Each hole is worth a skin. The player or team with the lowest score wins the hole and the skin. If the hole is a tie then the skin is 'pushed' to the next hole making the next hole worth two skins. The player or team with the most skins after 18 holes wins.
